Project Title

Background

Spotify is a music streaming service that allows users to scan through a large library of songs from a variety of genres. This music service has both a mobile app and a desktop app. Other features include collaboration on playlists with other users, recommendations for the user based on songs, and access to the user’s friend activity.

Goals:

1. Recruit and conduct user research to determine users' pain points while using Spotify.
2. Brainstorm ideas with my UX design team for new Spotify features.
3. Create working prototypes for the two new features.

I worked with another UX designer on this project. My role was a UX researcher and designer. The project took approximately 4 weeks.

Process

Market Research (Competitive Analysis) --> User Research (User Personas, Surveys) --> Paper Prototypes (Mobile Sketches) --> Iterations --> Low-Fidelity Wireframes --> Iterations --> Mid-Fidelity Wireframes --> Visual Design (Moodboard + Style Guide) --> High-Fidelity Wireframes --> Prototyping --> Usability Testing --> Iterations --> Presentations --> Develop

  • Research

  • Create
  • Iterate

Research

Research consisted of market research using competitive analysis, user personas, user research with surveys and usability testing.

Ideate

Our idea was to improve on Spotify's current user interface.

Create and Iterate

We created sketches of both mobile and desktop experiences. After creating the sketches, we created low-fidelity wireframes, iterated, created mid-fidelity wireframes, iterated, then finally created the high-fidelity wireframes.

Present

We presented our findings to our stakeholders and gave them recommendations for implementation.

Develop

We developed the design into a prototype and implemented it on a hosting website.
